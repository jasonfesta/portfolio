The importance of simple software

Why software that does less, but better, almost always wins — and how to keep simplicity intact under shipping pressure.

January 21, 2026 · 3 min read

Simple software is easier to reason about, easier to maintain, and easier to trust.

Complexity often arrives one reasonable decision at a time. Without constraints, it compounds silently.

Teams that protect simplicity move faster over the long run because every change has less surface area.
