The Managed Agent Network

An argument for an identity-bound, accountable network of agents — and why the most important software product of the next ten years will not be open.

April 28, 2026 · 6 min read

A few months ago, the agent demos were everywhere. But there is a difference between an impressive demo and trusted daily utility.

The product challenge is not just model quality. It is identity, accountability, and confidence in who is acting on your behalf.

The network that wins will likely be managed: clear ownership, explicit trust boundaries, and stronger guarantees when things fail.
