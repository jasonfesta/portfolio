Rubbrband (YC)

Fal, Cursor, Remotion, FFmpeg

Project

Role

Social content produced for Rubbrband as part of YC. We place founders and team members — real people, real stories — inside cinematic, high-concept set designs they'd never have the budget or occasion to commission on their own. Each scene is built around a quote from the subject, worked directly into the world of the set, so the piece feels less like a marketing asset and more like a short film: our heroes given the production value they've earned but never had access to. Built using Fal, Cursor, Remotion, and FFmpeg.
