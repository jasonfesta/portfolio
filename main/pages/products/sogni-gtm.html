Sogni AI

Figma, Fal, Sogni, Midjourney

Project

Role

Led creative direction and design for Sogni AI, a decentralized creative platform giving users access to over 100 image, video, music, and language models, all under one private, community-powered network, and led the branding and go-to-market launch that drove 20M views and 150M generations. The work spanned the visual identity across Sogni's suite of Super Apps, from Studio and Create to Pocket and Restore, alongside the campaign built to introduce the Supernet and its unrestricted, model-agnostic approach to creative AI to a mainstream audience, turning a technical, decentralized product into something that broke through culturally at launch.

Brand

Design

Generations

Led the creation of over 300 AI-generated GIFs for launch, a rapid-fire content push designed to make Sogni's capabilities tangible and shareable rather than abstract. The volume and variety let the launch spread far beyond a single campaign push, driving 20M+ views and getting picked up and shared by 50+ partners, turning the GIF library itself into a distribution engine that carried the brand across platforms and communities on its own.
