AI UGC

Fal, Cursor, Remotion, FFmpeg

Project

Role

Built AI UGC virality systems at scale, engineering a pipeline that identifies viral videos in near real time, scanning and pulling down fresh hits on an hourly, sometimes minute-by-minute basis. Each video is then dissected scene by scene using face and edge detection, isolating exactly what made it work, before those elements are fed back into the pipeline with new creators to generate endless variations on the original hook, all without touching a single third-party service or UI.

Generations

The entire system runs on custom-built methods stitched together from Fal, Cursor, Remotion, and FFmpeg, from model orchestration and code generation to motion graphics and video assembly, giving full control over every step of the pipeline rather than relying on off-the-shelf tools. That control is what makes the system capable of running unattended at scale, sourcing, deconstructing, and redeploying viral formats with new faces and voices continuously, on its own schedule, with no manual intervention required.
