OpenSea

Veo, Hailuo, Udio, Figma, After Effects, Topaz

Project

Role

Created one of a series of Italian brainrot-style AI videos to jam OpenSea Voyages and its NFT royalty IP straight into internet culture. This one follows a totally cooked OS lobster, tricorn hat, captain's wheel fused to his head, glitch-dancing and sailing his way to OpenSea Island with a crew of messed-up animal-craft hybrids: paddleboard penguins, jet-ski apes, boat-bodied seals. The goal was to turn the royalty NFT IP into scroll-breaking mascots dumb enough to make people stop, laugh, and wonder what they just watched, dumb on purpose, because that's the point. Built using Midjourney, Veo, Hailuo AI, Udio, Figma, After Effects, and Topaz.
