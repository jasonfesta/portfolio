Wayve

Figma, Fal, Cursor, Jitter, Remotion

Project

Role

Led creative direction across all of design for Wayve, one of the very first iOS applications on the App Store to generate fully personalized AI video batches for every user, every day. Each batch was built entirely around that individual, short ~30-second videos, each centered on its own story and beat, generated specifically for them, with no input required beyond simply using the app.

Design

Generations

Every day, the Wayve iOS app delivered a batch of five personalized videos built entirely around the individual user, spanning a mix of short-form vertical formats from memes and news to music videos and broader entertainment, so each batch felt less like a feed and more like a daily reel curated for an audience of one.
