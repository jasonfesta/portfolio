AI Ads

Fal, Cursor, Remotion, FFmpeg

Project

Role

Embedded AI Ads use AI to insert a brand's product directly into video content that's already been shot, no reshoot, script, or shipped product required, applied across platforms like YouTube, TikTok, and Instagram at once. What makes it novel is that the placement blends into the scene instead of interrupting it, so it earns attention rather than getting skipped, while scaling to thousands of creators and tens of thousands of ad slots at once, far beyond what a traditional sponsorship deal could reach.

Generations

I helped design and battle test Protoballs, a complex AI system built with the development team to communicate directly with every generation model available in the world via MCP, orchestrating image and video generation through Cursor and Remotion alongside custom FFmpeg tooling to produce hundreds of AI ads at scale. Along the way, built a suite of tools spanning a variety of video forms, including both UGC and AI-generated content, guiding proprietary methods for searching, stitching, and detecting faces across hundreds of minutes of vertical video to turn raw model output into finished, on-brand ads with minimal manual editing.
